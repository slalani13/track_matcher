Casual This is an untimed mode that plays like anagrams. The goal is to unscramble all words of a given track for a specific artist. You can skip tracks you do not know.
Competitive This is a timed and scored mode that plays like hangman. The goal is to fill in the first 3 words of a given track of a randomly selected artist. You have two different classes that reveal characters differently. You can skip tracks giving you trouble to maximize score. You can input your score into a local leaderboard after.
